About 26 January

26 January is celebrated as Republic Day in India because the Constitution of India came into effect on this day in 1950. It marks the day when India became a sovereign, democratic republic.

Facts about Republic Day

  • India became a republic on 26 January 1950.
  • The Constitution of India came into force on this day.
  • A grand parade is held in New Delhi every year.
  • It is a national holiday in India.
